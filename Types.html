Not every person with ADHD are the same. It depends on the type of person with the disorder and their surroundings while going through life.

    ADHD-I

  • People with this subtype are often distracted and have trouble paying attention, but don't show HyperActivity

    • ADHD-HI

  • People with this subtype are often hyperactive but don't have problems with paying attention.

    • ADHD-C

  • This is the most common type. Individuals with this form suffer from both problems with hyperactivity and attention deficits.
